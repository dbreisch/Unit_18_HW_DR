My UX Skills

User Research

I'm a baller researcher. Having worked in operations, I'm comfortable manipulating qualitative and quantitative data. and allowing them to drive decision-making. I conduct interviews and sift through the data to create personas, journeys and more.

User Interface Design

I bring a practical and professional approach to UI design. I work from the ground up, starting with sketches and lo-fi wireframes. From there I think deeply but swiftly about what components and local styles will facilitate my team's work. And I iterate all the way through the hi-fi.

Web Development

A solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and experience with Bootstrap helps me communicate and empathize with developers. I believe the UX Design process is a two-way street.
